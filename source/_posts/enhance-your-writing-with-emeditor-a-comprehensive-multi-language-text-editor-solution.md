 2\. click on A1  
 3\. Type line1

 5\. Type line2  
 6\. Press the ALT-Key, and hit the ENTER key  
 7\. Type line3  
 8\. Click on B1  
 9\. Type line1 hit ENTER  
 10\. Type line2 hit ENTER
